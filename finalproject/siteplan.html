Project Site Plan

Site Name:

Local Weather

Site Purpose:

Provide local weather related information.

  • 1. Current Weather
  • 2. 5 day forecast
  • 3. Weather alerts
  • 4. Doppler/map
  • 5. UV/Air quality
  • 6. Historical temps

I live in a desert and in the direct line of air flow from fires in another state. Plus we get a lot of random storms

Scenarios:

The user needs to provide the city or zip code for the area they want info for. The browswer will try to use their local location first.
